What is Alpha Ghost Camouflage?

Created by Alpha Dynamic Concepts (ADC) in the ecologically diverse Colorado Rockies, Alpha Ghost Camouflage is a next generation system of interactive camouflage technology with optimal performance characteristics. Our patterns are scientifically designed to conceal in a wider range of distances and a broader spectrum of light conditions, resulting in the capability to conceal in both high and low light environments, simultaneously.

Our team at Alpha Dynamic Concepts (ADC) has vastly expanded knowledge of the camouflage science through countless hours of cross-disciplinary applied research and development, using a creative, unconventional mindset. Our primary focus thus far has been directed towards military applications. ADC is now beginning to pursue business opportunities in the hunting and commercial industries. We will deliver the absolute best camouflage system available.

CAMOUFLAGE PATTERNS

Our design team can easily and rapidly modify patterns to conceal in any specific environment necessary, which opens the door to unlimited marketing possibilities. Our patterns can be modified to suit market or tactical needs in any terrain.

  • Natural Environment Matching Fractal Patterns Customizable to Any Environment (Broad or Specific) - No Guesswork
  • Commercial/Hunting Variations - Urban/Alpine/Mountain/Desert/Jungle/ Field, etc.
  • Law Enforcement/Military Capable Variations
  • Very Aesthetically Appealing for Marketability
  • Simultaneously Reduces Contrast in Low Light Environments and Daylight Environments Across a Broad Spectral Region
  • Streetwear, Swimwear, Ski/Snowboard Wear, etc.
