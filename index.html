What is Alpha Ghost Camouflage?

Next Gen Camouflage

Created by Alpha Dynamic Concepts (ADC) in the ecologically diverse Colorado Rockies, Alpha Ghost Camouflage is a next generation system of camouflage technology with optimal performance characteristics. Our patterns are scientifically designed to conceal in a wider range of distances and a broader spectrum of light conditions. The patterns used are fractal based and mimic nature at the elemental level.

Camouflage Patterns

Within the Alpha Ghost Camouflage line are planned environmental subsets such as Mountain Ghost™ - Urban Ghost™ - Desert Ghost™ - and more! Our design team can easily and rapidly modify patterns to conceal in any specific environment necessary, which opens the door to unlimited marketing possibilities. Our patterns can be modified to suit market needs in any terrain.
